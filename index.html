About me

I am a PhD astrophysics student at Max Planck Institute for Radio Astronomy. My research investigates the physical mechanisms governing star formation, focusing on molecular cloud morphology and the impact of stellar feedback on molecular gas. I employ observations, simulations and machine learning tools to explore the stellar feedback regions in the Galaxy.
